About Introduction Inventing Self is designed to help students discover their authentic selves and what they wish to pursue in life. In other words, INVENTING SELF helps student lay the foundation for finding happiness in life. Getting Started The curriculum is student guided, that as students develop ideas and interests to pursue and implement, that drives the instructions. Thus, the goal of developing leadership skills is not rhetoric, but reality. The cirriculum is designed to guide students toward developing a solid foundation for self-esteem, confidence, introspection, interpersonal connections and creativity... oh yeah, and happiness.
