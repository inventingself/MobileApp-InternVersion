Take5

Close your eyes.

Place your right index finger in the center of your left palm

As you inhale, trace gently from the center of the palm to the tip of of your thumb

Exhale, trace back down to the center of your palm.

Inhale, trace up to the tip of your index finger.

Exhale, trace back to the center of your palm.

Inhale, trace up the middle finger,

Exhale down back to center.

Inhale to the tip of the ring finger.

Exhale back to center.

Inhale up to the tip of your pinky.

Exhale back to the center of your palm.

*To "take 10", repeat with the right palm and the left index finger.
